What Is CoDonate?

CoDonate is a platform which lets you donate to NGOs who are fighting COVID-19 all around the world using more than ten cryptocurrencies.

How It Works

From the Donate page select the cryptocurrency you would like to donate in and send the desired amount to our wallet address. We will automatically donate them to our partner organizations.

What Is the Benefit?

If you are unsure about which NGO to donate and want to donate using your cryptocurrencies we are the only platform allowing these kind of donations.
